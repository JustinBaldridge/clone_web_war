Beliefs!

Leadership

Leadership is a very important thing in groups, but not to use their powers against their team members. A group enviroment is not cause for overpowering another. It is all about working together, and accepting your idea may not be the greatest. Leadership isn't about command or control, fear or force, demand or denial. It's about respect and responsibility.
